Our Design Process

1. Consultation & Discovery

We begin by understanding your vision, needs, and style preferences through a detailed consultation. Whether in-person or virtual, this step helps us align with your goals and lifestyle.

2. Concept Development

Our team translates your ideas into a creative concept, complete with mood boards, sketches, and material samples. We refine the design until it perfectly reflects your desired aesthetic and functionality.

3. Design Planning

Once the concept is approved, we create detailed floor plans, layouts, and 3D renderings. This allows you to visualize the final design and ensures every element is planned with precision.

4. Sourcing & Procurement

We manage the selection and purchase of all furniture, materials, and finishes. Our industry partnerships give you access to exclusive pieces and the highest quality products.

5. Project Management

We oversee every aspect of the installation and construction process, coordinating with contractors and tradespeople to ensure timelines are met and the design is executed flawlessly.

    • Some of Your Questions

    How involved will I be in the design process?

    We believe in a collaborative approach to design. You can be as involved as you like! From the initial consultation to final styling, we'll ensure your input is valued every step of the way. While we'll handle all the technical details, we'll keep you informed and invite your feedback to make sure the final design reflects your vision perfectly.

    I'm concerned about staying within my budget. How do you manage costs?

    We understand the importance of working within a budget. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget in detail and design a plan that meets your financial expectations without compromising on quality. We're experienced in finding creative solutions and sourcing products that fit within a range of price points, ensuring transparency and no surprises.

    What if I don't like the design?

    Our process is designed to prevent this! We'll present you with mood boards, sketches, and 3D renderings so you can visualize the design before anything is finalized. We work closely with you to make revisions at the concept stage, ensuring that you're fully satisfied before we move into the implementation phase. Your happiness with the design is our top priority.

    How long will the project take?

    Every project is unique, but we'll provide you with a detailed timeline during the planning stage. We pride ourselves on efficient project management and clear communication, so you'll always know what to expect. Our goal is to deliver your dream space on time, and we work closely with all contractors to stay on schedule.
